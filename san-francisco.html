Where to find us

Prezi's San Francisco office is located in the heart of the famous SOMA district, home of myriad innovative startups and buzzworthy companies. Bars and restaurants surround us on all sides, as well as parks, fountains, shopping, and art studios.

Our open meetup space offers room for our growing team of passionate "Prezilians," who have everything they need for creative and productive work, including delicious and healthy food, ping pong, a shelf full of board games, and yeah, a bar.

Rolling up our sleeves to help our community

We love where we live, and do all we can to help keep it a vibrant, beautiful place. Here are just some of the ways we try to pitch in.

Renovation project

Each year, our San Francisco office spends a day building, sweating, and helping out with the youth at Seven Tepees, an organization serving the City's most under-resourced youth through intensive support programs and services.

Women in tech

We're proud to be working with Latinas in Tech, whose more-than-3,000 members empower and mentor female innovators in more than 15 countries.
