Where to find us

The heart of Prezi lies at the beating heart of Europe, in the beautiful city of Budapest. With the burgeoning tech startup scene in full bloom, the Pearl of the Danube has become the new European hub for innovation and technology.

This city is steeped in rich historic heritage, but is vibrant and young at the same time, a place where lovely cafes spring up alongside crumbling castle walls and some of the most cutting edge tech work is done in buildings that were already ancient by the time electricity was discovered.
