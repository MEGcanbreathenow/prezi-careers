Where to find us

Prezi's office in Riga is the home of our Infogram team. It's located in the historic Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site where cobbled fairytale lanes bustle with cafes, bars, restaurants, and shops for every taste.

We're on the penthouse floor of our building, with a rooftop terrace for early morning coffees or after-work refreshments. Inside, you'll find an open-office layout with designated work zones, chill zones, mini basketball, healthy snacks, and lots of other cool stuff to keep you happy and energized.

Rolling up our sleeves to help our community

We love where we live, and do all we can to help keep it a vibrant, beautiful place. Here are just some of the ways we try to pitch in.

Renovation project

The Infogram team is proud to partner with SOS Children's Villages, a global initiative to help kids who've lost parental care or are at-risk for it. This year, we'll be helping them relocate to a new settlement and renovate their area.

Women in tech

At Infogram, we’ve always been “women in tech,” as embodied by our co-founder, Alise Dirka. Alise leads the project Riga TechGirls, educating and inspiring young women all over Latvia to get into the exciting world of technology.
